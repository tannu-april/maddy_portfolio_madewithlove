Through the Lens of Passion

This isn’t just a portfolio. This is a piece of you — wrapped in light, memories, and all the little in-betweens you’ve captured over time. You started off chasing a dream that was never really yours — civil engineering. And even though it wasn’t meant for you, you gave it your best. But deep down, you always knew you were built differently — a little strange, a little quiet, endlessly curious, and full of stories waiting to be told. You didn’t run away from that path, you gently walked away — and straight into the arms of something you loved: Photography. Creating reels that speak without a script. That’s where your spirit came alive. And the way you’ve embraced it — with every shoot, every frame, every heartbeat — is nothing short of inspiring. You’ve turned your lens into a bridge — between moments and meaning. And your favorite tool? That Google Pixel. It’s not just a phone in your hands; it’s magic. You’ve got this wild ability to make the simplest things look cinematic — like the world pauses when you click. Outside the frames, you’re still a storyteller. A gamer. A wanderer. A dreamer. Someone who feels deeply and sees the world just a little differently. You don’t just record moments, you feel them. You remember how the sky looked, how the wind moved, the way laughter sounded. And that’s why I made this. Because everything you create deserves to be seen. Because you deserve to be celebrated. Because in your silence, your art, your chaos — you revive me. Happy Birthday, love. Keep creating. Keep chasing the light. The world needs to see what only you can capture.

Made with endless love and stardust ✨

For you, on your birthday — my favorite muse.
